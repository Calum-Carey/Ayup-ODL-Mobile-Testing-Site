Info, booking & more

What?

A not-for-profit resource for the digital community and forms part of the global Open Device Lab network. Test the speed, functionality, interactivity and performance of your digital project and ensure it works and is accessible to all!

Booking

We warmly welcome local individuals (freelancers etc) to make us of the device lab, however bookings must be made so we can properly manage usage and scheduling.
